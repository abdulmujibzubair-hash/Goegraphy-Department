Our Academic Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to equip students with both theoretical and practical knowledge of geography and environmental management.

B.Sc. Geography & Environmental Management

Our undergraduate program focuses on environmental systems, spatial data analysis, and sustainable development.

M.Sc. Geography

Advanced research-based learning for students interested in spatial analysis, climate studies, and policy planning.

Ph.D. Geography

Doctoral-level research in geography and environmental management under experienced academic supervision.
